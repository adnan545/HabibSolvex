Refining Process

Our multi-step refining process ensures the highest quality and purity.

1. Sourcing

Premium sunflower, soybean, and palm sourced from trusted farms.

2. Degumming

Removal of phospholipids and gums for clarity.

3. Neutralization

Free fatty acids removed using caustic soda.

4. Bleaching & Deodorization

Color removal and odor neutralization.

5. Packaging

Automated filling in food-grade containers.

6. Quality Checks

Every batch tested in our NABL-accredited lab.
